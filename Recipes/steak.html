Classic Grilled Steak

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Take out the steak from the fridge and let it sit at room temperature for 30 minutes
  2. Season the steak with salt, pepper, and garlic powder
  3. Preheat your grill to high heat
  4. Grill the steak for about 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare
  5. Let the steak rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving
