Beef Burger with cheesse

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Cheese
  • Bun
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup fine dry bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F
  2. Mix all ingredients
  3. Put it on the pan
  4. Put it on the oven
  5. Wait 10 minutes
  6. Eat it
