pepperoni pizza with cheesse

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Dough
  • Tomato
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Cheese
  • Fresh basil
  • Onion
  • Red pepper
  • Mozzarella
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Gather the necessary ingredients and tools.
  2. Preheat the oven to 450°F (235°C)
  3. Prepare the dough: roll out the dough and spread it on a baking sheet.
  4. Make the sauce: combine the tomato sauce, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a saucepan.Bake the dough: place the dough in the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown.
  5. Add the toppings: spread your desired toppings on top of the dough.
  6. Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly: put the pizza in the oven and continue baking for 8-12 minutes.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  8. Enjoy your homemade pizza!
