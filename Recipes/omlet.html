Omelette

Classic Omelette

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter or oil
  • Optional fillings (e.g., cheese, vegetables, ham)

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them with salt and pepper
  2. Heat the butter or oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat
  3. Pour the beaten eggs into the pan and let them cook undisturbed for a minute
  4. Add any optional fillings on one half of the omelette
  5. Use a spatula to fold the omelette in half and cook for another minute
  6. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve immediately
